Our Mission

Using Zoom for education has become an unfortunate reality, causing millions of people struggle with organizing and teaching over the platform. Many of the difficulties that come with this can only be solved by a return to classes, but some of its challenges can be solved today.

That’s why we created Ease Attendance, a free and easy way to automatically track attendance of Zoom participants and students. After just a few minutes of setup, you’ll never have to worry about tracking when or if participants arrive, as our website will show you exactly who came and when they came to your meetings in a clean, easy to use, dashboard. All you’ll have to do is either upload a roster of students or create a class by importing participants from a Zoom meeting.

We want to streamline your online classroom experience to focus on teaching, and to not worry on the details. Ease Attendance will be updated and improved with user feedback to make it as useful as possible to our users.
