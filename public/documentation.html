Getting Started

Prerequisites

  1. Have separate scheduled Zoom meetings for each class. Ease Attendance keeps track of expected participants for
    each meeting, and will compare the actual participants with the expected participants to check attendance.
  2. For the best experience, have students log into classes with their full name, ex. “John Doe”. This will allow the sort by
    first/last names to work most effectively.
  3. Inform students to not change displayed Zoom names, or else Ease Attendance might miss their attendance.

Step 1 - Install Zoom App

  1. Click the Add to Zoom button to install the app
    2. Add to Zoom

Step 2 - Sign Up

  1. Go to Ease Attendance and click Sign Up

    Note: You must install the Zoom App before signing up

  2. Enter in your credentials.

    Note: Make sure the email you enter is the same as the email you use to sign in to Zoom

  3. Verify your email
  4. After verifying your email, you will be redirected to the Ease Attendance Dashboard

Step 3 - Add Class Roster

  • Automatic

    1. Start a Zoom Meetings and watch participants be displayed on Ease Attendance as they join.
    2. When the participants finish coming into the Zoom meeting, click on update participants in the
      Ease Attendance dashboard to store them in a Meeting Roster.
    3. Confirm meeting details and click finish. Go to the My Meetings tab to confirm that your meeting has been created

  • Manual

    Note: This action will not create a meeting for you on Zoom but instead registers the following as a class roster on Ease Attendance

    1. Go to the My Meetings tab
    2. Click on add meeting
    3. Input your Zoom meeting details and add students to your class roster
    4. Confirm meeting details and click finish. Now you'll be able to see your newly-created meeting.

Step 4 - Start Meeting

  1. Start any meeting and watch your class attendance update live!

Uninstalling

Step 1 - Login in to Zoom Marketplace

  1. Go to the Zoom Marketplace and login

Step 2 - Locate App

  1. Click on Manage > Installed Apps and locate Ease Attendance

Step 3 - Uninstall

  1. Click on uninstall

    Note: All account data stored on Ease Attendance will be deleted automatically
