Getting Started

Step 1 - Install Zoom App

  1. Go to the Zoom Marketplace
  2. Search Ease Attendance and install

Step 2 - Sign Up

  1. Go to Ease Attendance and click Sign Up
  2. Enter in your credentials.

    Note: Make sure the email you enter is the same as the email you use to sign in to Zoom

  3. Verify your email

Step 3 - Add Class Roster

  • Automatic

    1. Start a meeting
    2. Complete the meeting a wait for the add meting pop up
    3. Confirm meeting details and click finish. Go to the My Meetings tab to confirm that your meeting has been created

  • Manual

    1. Go to the My Meetings tab
    2. Click on add meeting
    3. Input your Zoom meeting details and add students to your class roster
    4. Confirm meeting details and click finish

      Note: This action will not create a meeting for you on Zoom but instead registers the following as a class roster on our website

Step 4 - Start Meeting

  1. Start any meeting and watch you class attendance update live

Uninstalling

Step 1 - Login in to Zoom Marketplace

  1. Go to the Zoom Marketplace and login

Step 2 - Locate App

  1. Click on Manage > Installed Apps and locate Ease Attendance

Step 3 - Uninstall

  1. Click on uninstall

    Note: All account data stored on Ease Attendance will be deleted automatically
